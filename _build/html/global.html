Weather and climate-related disasters have made headlines around the world this year. This section takes a closer look at global climate conditions and significant weather events.

The plots below will be updated each month. At the bottom of this page you will also find a map showing the locations of some of the most extreme weather events of the year.

World Extreme Weather Events in 2023#

Below is a map highlighting some of the most extreme weather events so far this year. This includes extreme heatwaves, powerful storms, flooding, and large intense wildfires. Combined, these events have resulted in thousands of lives lost and homes destroyed, and have cost billions in response and recovery.

You can hover over the icons on the map to see the name and date of the event, and click on the icons for more information.
